In yay or paru, after you upgrade a package, it is up to you to rebuild it when [needed](https://bbs.archlinux.org/viewtopic.php?id=294674). This is the case for sioyek. Below you can find the command to rebuild the package.


<!--more-->

# The solution

The command to rebuil sioyek is
```
yay --rebuild sioyek
```
